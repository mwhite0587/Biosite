About Alleyah

Alleyah, a vibrant and creative teenage girl with a love for Hello Kitty, drawing, and baking, is known for her cheerful personality and her passion for all things pink. Her dream of becoming an aerospace engineer is fueled by her favorite pastime of sketching cute characters and adorning her room with Hello Kitty collectibles. A dedicated student, Alleyah recently received a school award for creativity, showcasing her unique style and joyful spirit.

Born after a challenging 26 hours of labor on March 7, 2009, Alleyah Angela-McKenzie Flint entered the world with bright eyes and a spirited demeanor. Her early alertness and curiosity were evident as she gazed into her mother's eyes, displaying a keen awareness of her surroundings. At just 9 months old, Alleyah's remarkable ability to recognize and point to words hinted at her precocious nature.

From a young age, Alleyah's love for music, particularly classical melodies, shaped her days spent listening under the sun. Her passion for patterns, numbers, and mathematics emerged early, leading her to express a desire to learn the piano at the age of 3. Despite the challenge of finding a suitable teacher, Alleyah's mother nurtured her passion, eventually connecting her with a dedicated piano instructor. Through dedication and talent, Alleyah progressed from mastering the basics to playing advanced pieces, a skill she continues to refine at 16.

Today, as a teenager at Duchesne Academy, Alleyah embodies brightness, beauty, and intelligence. Her commitment to academic excellence reflects her aspirations of securing a place in a reputable college, driven by her dream of pursuing a career as an aerospace engineer.
